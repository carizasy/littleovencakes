ABOUT US





Little Oven Cakes started as a hobby way back in 2007. Sasa, the founder and owner, started taking orders of her friends, family and colleagues and baking it as a favor. Unexpectedly, the orders soon started flooding in and she thought why not make make it a business. With her love for baking and passion for the finest, fresh ingredients the online business grew from strength to strength.
